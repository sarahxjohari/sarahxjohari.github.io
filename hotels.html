Hotels

Come stay with us in New York City!

We have room blocks reserved at three nearby hotels. Reservations can be made through links included below or by calling the hotel directly and giving our group code.

We also have a link to group rates at a lot of different hotels through Hotels.com, but if you're interested in the three below you should book through our code/link instead for a better rate.
