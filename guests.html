For Guests Coming from Out of Town

We are so thankful for our family and friends coming from far away to celebrate with us. We've selected hotels near the wedding venue that you can get special rates on, so we hope you can easily get to us or explore the city!

Getting Around

The easiest way to get to the venue from the hotels we've chosen is to walk or take a cab; they range from a 6 to 17 minute walk, or less than 10 minute cab ride in lighter traffic. (As always in New York, cabs may take longer than one might hope during busy times!)

For the two slightly further hotels you can also very easily take the R or W train on the subway downtown to Whitehall Street in about 8 minutes. The 4, 5 and 1 trains also stop near Battery Gardens, at Bowling Green (4, 5) and South Ferry (1), if you are staying somewhere different that's closer to those trains.

All of these locations and subway lines can be navigated using Google Maps if you want to chart a course.
